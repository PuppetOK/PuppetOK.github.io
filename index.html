What is Dark Souls?

Dark Souls is a series of video games developed by From Software, a Japanese developer. It is widely known for being difficult and for not "holding" the players hand through the game. There are a total of 3 Dark Souls games, and the preceeding Demon's Souls game.

Dark Souls Games

  1. Demon's Souls
  2. Dark Souls
  3. Dark Souls II
  4. Dark Souls III
